Interpreting the Survey:

Each factor of the Big 5 Personality (Extraversion, Agreeableness, Conscientiousness, Emotional Stability, and Openness to Experience) has a range of possible scores from 5-50. The higher the score, the more you perceive this characteristic as descriptive of your personality.
